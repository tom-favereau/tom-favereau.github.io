Twin prime conjecture and sensitivity

Fixed

About this Cellular Automaton

This cellular automaton exhibits sensitivity to initial conditions if and only if the Twin Prime Conjecture is true. This is a consequence of the fact that sensitivity to initial conditions in 1D cellular automata is a Π⁰₂ property.

Interested readers should refer to this article where we prove (with Ville Salo) the complexity of sensitivity for cellular automata and its implications.
